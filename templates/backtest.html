Backtest

Windows: 1-month · 1-year · 5-year · composite-score signal

Interpretation. A positive BUY minus SELL spread means BUY-signaled bars outperformed SELL-signaled bars on average — the core proof-of-concept test. Accuracy measures how often BUY→positive and SELL→negative forward returns. Cumulative strategy return compounds long/short/flat bets with zero transaction costs; compare it to buy-and-hold to see whether the signal added value. Fundamentals (P/E, DCF) are held at their current values across the window; RSI and price momentum are fully historical.
